About me

I design
with intention

I'm Lucius — a front-end designer based in Nairobi with a passion for the space where design and code meet. I believe great interfaces don't just function; they communicate, they breathe, they feel right.

My work spans brand identities, web applications, and mobile interfaces. I obsess over typography, motion, and the subtle details that turn a good UI into an unforgettable one.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm creating content about technology, AI, and the future of design — always exploring, always building.
