Our story

Rooted in tradition

Séchoir was born from a simple belief — that African cuisine deserves the same reverence, craft, and creativity as any culinary tradition in the world. Our kitchen sources from small farms, coastal fishers, and forest foragers across East Africa.

Every dish is a conversation between memory and imagination. We don't reinvent African food. We honour it — and then ask: what's next?

Chef Amina Waweru
Executive Chef & Co-founder
